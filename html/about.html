About This Page


Generally speaking, this web site is a means for me to share with the world. Whether that's sharing my thoughts on random articles or ideas, or sharing material that I've found interesting ... I pretty much just post whatever I want. I intend for visitors of my web site to gain one (or more) of the following:


  1. New knowledge on a subject
  2. New perspective on a subject
  3. A desire to connect with me
  4. The conviction that your visit was not a complete waste of time!

The 'home' page of this web site is my Blog. I hope to update this on a weekly basis. The left-hand side bar contains links to various tutorials and material that I feel is worth looking at outside of blog posts. This is updated sporadically as time allows.

I also provide my Curriculum Vitae for anyone (especially recruiters) who may be interested in me professionally.

Lastly, should you feel the need to contact me, I warmly welcome any new connections.
